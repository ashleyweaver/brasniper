About BraSniper

BraSniper was created by Ashley Weaver at PennApps Spring 2014

I created this site because I wanted to automate my search for the perfect bra. Because my bra size is not often sold in stores, I have to buy most of my bras online. However, this can get costly - bras can be very expensive and may need to be shipped from overseas. The subreddit /r/braswap helps with this by connecting buyers and sellers. My website will email a user from bot@brasniper.com when a new thread is posted selling a bra in their size.
